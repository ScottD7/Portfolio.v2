SAMSUNG: Going Smart

Samsung is reaching out to various company's such as Chamberlain, Simplisafe, and LiftMaster to promote upcoming and smart home features. There are so many options out there currently for creating the perfect smart home. The creators at Samsung have relized this and are now working with smaller companies. In an effort to create a single app that can communicate with all the smart devices. The app is still in Alpha however in efforts to create communication. This site offers all the smart phone apps offered between companies. All reviews for each app will be monitored and passed directly to the developers at Samsung.

Our mission is to make a family friendly application, that all ages can learn and master quickly. Parental controls will be a large factor of the new application.
